Carbon Emissions Calculator

EcoTracker integrates the Carbon Interface API to allow users to calculate carbon emissions for common activities such as driving, flying, or household energy usage. Users input parameters like distance traveled, fuel type, or energy consumption, and the application provides estimates of CO2 emissions associated with those activities.

Personal Carbon Footprint Tracker

EcoTracker provides users with a personal carbon footprint tracker that aggregates data from their calculated emissions over time. Users can input their activities regularly, track their carbon footprint trends, set reduction goals, and receive personalized recommendations for adopting more eco-friendly habits.

Environmental Impact Comparison Tool

EcoTracker enables users to compare the environmental impact of different lifestyle choices and activities. By inputting parameters for various options (e.g., driving vs. cycling, eating meat vs. plant-based diet), users can see the corresponding carbon emissions estimates and make informed decisions to minimize their environmental footprint.
